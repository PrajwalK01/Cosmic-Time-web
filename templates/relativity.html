The Geometry of Time

How Speed and Gravity Bend the Clock

3. Velocity Time Dilation

According to Einstein's Special Relativity, time slows down as you move faster. This is not a mechanical error of the clock, but a fundamental property of spacetime geometry. The "present" is subjective to your velocity.

Formula:

Δt' = Δt / √(1 − v² / c²)

4. Gravitational Time Dilation

According to General Relativity, time flows slower closer to massive objects. Near a black hole, a few hours could equal years for a distant observer. Gravity is not just a force, but the curvature of spacetime itself.

Formula:

Δt' = Δt √(1 − 2GM / rc²)
